Hi there! This is an everyday task: waste sorting! Your job here is to correctly separate rubbish.

Every element has a double-face card, if you're not sure where to put it, remember to click on it and read the description.
The system is based on the Milan waste sorting. Do you need a refresh? Go here

How to play: first select the elements and then click on the trash bin that you need. Once that every element is sorted go on the next level.
If you want to restart the game, just click the button.
